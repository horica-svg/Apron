<p><b>Apron</b> is a mobile application developed with Flutter, designed to help users discover recipes, manage their dietary preferences, and organize their shopping. The app provides a seamless experience for browsing meals and keeping track of your pantry and shopping list.
</p>

## Main Features (for now)

- **User Authentication**: Secure registration and login using Firebase Authentication (Email & Password).
- **Pantry Management**: Keep track of the ingredients you have at home and receive recipe suggestions based on those ingredients.
- **Recipe Discovery**: Browse through a comprehensive list of recipes, organized by the ingredients in your pantry.
- **Favorites**: Mark recipes as favorites for quick and easy access.
- **Shopping List**: Manage a shopping list for necessary ingredients.
